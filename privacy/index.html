Rainbow Bible – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑12‑06

Rainbow Bible is a devotional companion for the King James Version (KJV). It offers interactive reading, themed verse widgets, favorites, and sharing so that scripture stays close throughout the day. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Summary

  • No account, no sign‑in, no subscriptions, no ads.
  • No analytics SDKs or third‑party trackers.
  • Reading preferences, favorites, and widget selections are stored locally on your device (including the iOS App Group used by the widget extension).
  • The core app works offline. Network access occurs only if you open web links (official site / this policy) or choose to send feedback email.

Data We Store On Your Device

The app may save information such as your current reading chapter, typography settings, favorites list, and widget configuration (selected verse, theme, and background color). These items are written to the app’s sandboxed storage (UserDefaults and the shared App Group so the Home Screen widget can read them) and are never uploaded to our servers.

Your control:

  • Manage favorites or widget choices directly inside the app.
  • Remove all stored data by deleting the app (this also clears the App Group container).

Widget Settings & App Extensions

To keep the Home Screen widget synchronized, the app writes the selected verse, theme, and background style into an iOS App Group container. This container is accessible only by the Rainbow Bible app and its official widget extension on your device. No information is transmitted to us or to third parties.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, precise location).
  • No access to contacts, photos, camera, microphone, calendar, or files.
  • No behavioral analytics, advertising identifiers, or third‑party SDK tracking.

Optional Email Feedback

Tapping “Send Feedback” in Settings opens your Mail app with a draft email to lidonghui11911@gmail.com. To help troubleshoot, the draft includes the app version, iOS version, and device model. You can review or delete this information before sending. We only receive what you choose to send; the app never transmits data silently in the background.

In‑App Web Pages

Certain buttons may open our official website or this privacy policy in an in‑app browser. Visiting those pages may allow the website to collect standard server logs or cookies according to its own policy. The main reading and widget features remain fully offline.

Faith & Guidance Disclaimer

Rainbow Bible provides scripture readings for personal devotion and study. It does not offer medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. For any professional guidance, please consult qualified experts. Scripture reminders are inspirational tools, not certified counseling.

Children’s Privacy

The app is rated 4+ for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no account or personal identifiers are used, there is no way for us to identify individual users. If you believe a child has provided personal data via email, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

Your scripture history and preferences stay on your device within the iOS sandbox. We rely on iOS security protections; please keep your device updated and protected with a passcode or biometrics.

Data Retention

Local records remain on your device until you delete items or uninstall the app. We do not maintain any remote backup of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this notice when introducing new features (for example, additional widget styles). When we make material changes, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and publish the new version at this URL.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or Rainbow Bible, please email: lidonghui11911@gmail.com

Legal Notes

Rainbow Bible is a devotional reading aid. It does not replace professional advice in any discipline. Use the app as an inspirational companion and seek qualified professionals for medical, legal, financial, or psychological matters.
